    • Multilingual Support.
    To ensure that native speakers in Punjabi and Hindi can access all the information they need on the website and chatbot, in addition to English, we have incorporated language support for Punjabi and Hindi. This comprehensive language support will guarantee that native speakers do not miss out on any crucial information and can receive the assistance they require in their mother tongue. Furthermore, our speech recognition feature enables users to effortlessly communicate with the chatbot in their preferred language, enhancing the comfort of job seekers as they search for their desired positions.
